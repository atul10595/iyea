About Us

Overview

The Indian Youth Economic Association, a non-profit trust was founded by students in New Delhi in 2012. It has since, promoted economic research and the advancement of economic knowledge and understanding amongst the youth. It aims to spread economic knowledge and the results of economic research, encourage the exchange of ideas between the youth and the application of the results of economic research, support the education and training of students in economics and to provide resources for young economists in the areas of education, research, business, industry, government and public policy.
The Indian Youth Economic Association, apart from research, is also heavily invested in youth-based activities such as Debates, Quizzes and academic simulations such as Model UN. A known name amongst the youth of the country, The IYEA has been commended for its work with various Delhi University colleges and schools. The IYEA also conducts training workshops and sessions for schools and colleges for a wide variety of themes, including but not restricted to economics, finance, history, international and domestic politics, career development and counseling, leadership & personality growth and parliamentary debating. The IYEA has a history of strong tie-ups with over 40 esteemed academic and non-academic institutions. Vision & Mission

Aims and Objectives as per the charter of the Indian Youth Economic Association:

  • To spread economic knowledge and the results of economic research.
  • To encourage the exchange of ideas between the youth and the application of the results of economic research.
  • To support the education and training of students in economics.
  • To provide resources for young economists in the areas of education, research, business, industry, government and public policy.
