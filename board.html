Board of Trustees

Ajay Sabharwal

Ajay Sabharwal is the President of the IYEA and heads day-to- day operations from New Delhi. A student of Economics and Hisotry, Ajay also takes a keen interest in Politics & War Studies. A multi-faceted induvial, Ajay has been the driving force behind the growth and expansion of the IYEA.

Madhav Bharat Bhushan

Madhav Bharat Bhushan serves as Vice President, Finance. He completed his Bachelors in Management Studies from Delhi University and is now completing his Masters at Duke. Hailing from a family that is renowned in the financial services sector, Madhav is no less a financial wizard himself. He manages large and sturdy portfolios and is a champion of the derivatives market. He has served as the backbone of the organization since 2012.

Sarthak Garg

Sarthak Garg is Vice President for Logistics & Liaison. In the last year of his Chartered Accountancy Programme, Sarthak and has been a key asset to the IYEA. Having formerly served as the Head of the North Division and Treasurer of the IYEA, he now facilitates day-to-day operations and focuses on the expansion of the organization in different parts of the country.

Ashita Alag

Ashita Alag has served as the Secretary of the IYEA since its inception and is now serving as the Treasurer too. A student of Law and a humanitarian, Ashita has been the face of the social agendas & programmes of the IYEA. Ashita has been a silent and self-less work horse, having managed large events and conferences in the most efficient manners.

Neoma Dhingra

Neoma Dhingra serves as a Trustee of the IYEA. She is a graduate in Finance from the London School of Economics. She has also has an in-depth understanding in Accountancy and Economics. She has researched extensively on aspects dealing with her subjects of interested and has published several research papers. Working as an exectuvie in the banking sector, Neoma has been guding the Indian Youth Economic Association since its inception.

Sakshi Khanna

Sakshi Khanna serves as a Trustee of the IYEA. She completed her Bachelors in Chemical Engineering & Masters in Business Administration (MBA) degree and is an entrepreneur. Her business interests lie in the cosmetics industry, while she also keeps many social causes close to her heart.
